Grigoris Velegkas
CV
Hello! I am a final year PhD student in the Computer Science department at Yale University. My advisor is Amin Karbasi. My research focuses on learning theory and mechanism design. In summer 2023, I was a research intern at Google Research in Mountain View, hosted by Andres Perlroth and Gagan Aggarwal. From October 2023 until May 2024 I was a student researcher at Google Research hosted by Yuan Deng. In summer 2024, I was a research intern at Google Research in New York hosted by William Kong.

Before coming to Yale, I completed my undergrad studies at NTUA majoring in computer science and electrical engineering, where I worked with Dimitris Fotakis.
